Why RBL?

Business Interruption

More than 30 percent of businesses never reopen their doors after they’ve been closed down by a hurricane, tornado, flood or other devastating calamity. A business impact analysis should be developed to identify what an operation must do to protect itself in the face of a natural disaster. Even if your business escapes a disaster, there is still a risk that the business could suffer significant losses due to the inability of suppliers to deliver goods or services or a reduction in customers. Identify critical business activities and the resources needed to support them.
